Bio

Migos released their debut album Yung Rich Nation in July 2015, through Quality Control Music and 300 Entertainment. Their second album, Culture, was released in January 2017 also through both record labels, and debuted atop the US Billboard 200 chart.[2][3] The group later signed a deal with Motown and Capitol Records in February 2017,[4] and followed up the latter album with Culture II in January 2018, giving the group their second number one album in the United States.
